Services/Prices

Russian Manicure is included with every service to ensure the longevity of your nails! This is not an add on procedure, but will be included inthe price of the service.

Gel Manicure: $50

This is basic soft gel with solid color going over the natural nail.

Structured Gel Manicure: $70

This service is done on your natural nail with somethinG called builder gel or "hard gel". The viscosity of this product is for people who have thin nails, are active, or have the tendency to chip or peel their product off. This product will also help grow out your natural nail as it adds structure.

Acrylic: $70

This product is an enhancment that provides structure and is durable. I would suggest acrylic if you are a nail bitter or picker.

Dry Pedicure: $50, w/ color $60

A dry pedicure doesn't require a foot soak but does include a russian manicure/ exfohliation to make the feet appear tidy and well kept.
